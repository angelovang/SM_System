Monitor repairs

Our mission

Our mission is to be professionals, both in the servicing of equipment and in our relations with our customers. We strive to always provide quality repairs. Our main goal is for the customer to be satisfied and come back.

Our tim

Our team consists of ten highly qualified professionals in each of the areas in which we offer services.They can advise you on all questions related to your equipment, both on site in the service center and over the phone!

Warranty

For devices under warranty, we honor the manufacturer's warranty period. For devices repaired by us, the warranty period is from 6 months to 1 year, depending on the repaired part of the device.

Ecology

As an environmentally responsible electronics repair company, we ensure that end-of-life devices are properly recycled.Therefore, we offer you the opportunity to return your out-of-use devices, in exchange for which you will receive a discount for the spare parts used in the repair.
